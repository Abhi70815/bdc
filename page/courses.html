Our Courses

- Courses we offer-

BCA - Bachelor of Computer Applications

BCA is an undergraduate degree program that emphasizes computer science and its applications. It covers subjects related to programming languages, software development, database management, networking, and computer architecture.

BBA/B-COM

BBA is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on developing business and management skills. It covers subjects like accounting, finance, marketing, human resources, and operations management.

B.Sc

B.Sc is an undergraduate degree program that encompasses a wide range of subjects related to science. It includes various disciplines such as mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, computer science, and more. Each of these programs offers unique career opportunities and areas of specialization based on the subjects covered. It is essential to choose the program that aligns with your interests and career goals.

College Fees

-* The fees should be deposited in four installment:
* 1st installment at the time of admission in the respective year- 25% fees
* 2nd installment before the start of CT-1 of odd semester- 25% fees
* 3rd Installment at the start of even semester- 25% fees
* Last installment before the start of 1st CT of even semester- 25% fees
* Penalty is imposed after due date.

College Facilities

Library

The college library provides a wide range of books, journals, and online resources for students and faculty.
It is a quiet and comfortable space for studying and research.

Laboratory

The college laboratory is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities for conducting practical experiments and research in various fields of study.
Students can gain hands-on experience and enhance their learning.

The college offers a variety of sports facilities including a gymnasium, basketball court, football field, and swimming pool.
Students can participate in sports activities and stay fit.
